Tree Services

Keep your trees and shrubs healthy with expert services from Mueller Landscape Inc.

Our tree division is dedicated to providing expert tree care, from routine pruning and trimming to complex removals.

With a commitment to safety and customer satisfaction, we utilize the latest techniques and equipment to ensure the best results for your landscape. Trust us to enhance the natural beauty of your property while promoting the health and longevity of your trees.

Our tree services include:

  • Thinning, shaping, and crown reduction
  • Specialty palm trees
  • Fruit trees
  • Removals and stump grinding
  • Safety pruning
