Frequently Asked Questions

What are the services you provide?

Mueller Landscape Inc. offers the following services:

  • Lawn and Landscape Maintenance
  • Tree Trimming
  • Sprinkler Installation and Repair
  • Commercial Services

Custom services are available upon request.

Does it cost anything to set up a consultation?

No. We offer free consultations.

Where can I see pricing?

We have standard pricing included on our services page. For an accurate quote for your property, please visit our contact page and fill out the form. Someone from our team will contact you to schedule a free consultation and provide an accurate quote.

Do you provide sprinkler repair services?

Yes, we do. Please visit our sprinkler services page.

Is it possible to have service done today?

We may be able to accommodate your request for service today, contingent upon your location and crew availability in your area. To inquire about same-day service, please contact us at (619) 388-1212. Kindly note that our operating hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.

What is your billing and payment process?

We accept credit cards and checks for all services. We charge your credit card for the balance due at the end of the day we provide service. If paying by check, customers will leave a check each time service is provided.
